# Section 00 Goal: Course Introduction

## Goal
Set the narrative frame for the entire course. Students meet SkyLine Air, understand the journey from a 2 AM incident to an automated FinOps AI agent, and preview the toolchain they will build.

## Prerequisites
None — this is the first section.

## Video structure (1 video)
| Video | Focus | Time |
|-------|-------|------|
| **1** | Welcome to SkyLine Air FinOps Agent — course promise and arc | 3–4 min |


## Scope
- Introduce SkyLine Air as the fictional airline running on Kubernetes
- Preview the incident-driven learning arc (Section 02a)
- Outline the Collect → Analyze → Act pipeline students will build
- Name the toolchain: Kind, MCP, LangChain, issue tracker, Helm

## Out of scope
- Hands-on setup (Section 01)
- Technical deep dives (later sections)

## Success criteria
A learner can:
1. Explain what SkyLine Air is and why we use a fictional company
2. Describe the course arc: incident → FinOps pain → agent → production deployment
3. Name the five major phases of the pipeline they will build

# Section 02b Goal: Introduction to FinOps

## Goal
Bridge the emotional incident experience from Section 02a to the structured FinOps framework. Students learn what FinOps is, why AI agents change the game, and how the SkyLine Air blueprint maps to the agent they will build.

## Prerequisites
Complete Section 02a (Payment Gateway Down).

You should have experienced:
- The 2 AM incident narrative
- Symptom vs root cause debugging
- The ownership wall — missing labels, no team to page

## Video structure (3 videos)
| Video | Focus | Time |
|-------|-------|------|
| **1** | What is FinOps — Inform, Optimize, Operate | 3–4 min |
| **2** | FinOps for AI — why agents change FinOps now | 3–4 min |
| **3** | Blueprint for SkyLine Air — Collect, Analyze, Act | 3–4 min |

Transcripts: `transcript/1.md`, `transcript/2.md`, `transcript/3.md`

## Scope
- Define FinOps and the three phases (Inform, Optimize, Operate)
- Explain why AI agents are a FinOps force multiplier
- Map FinOps phases to the Collect → Analyze → Act agent pipeline
- Preview Sections 05–09 as the implementation path

## Out of scope
- Hands-on cluster work (Sections 01–03)
- Writing agent code (Sections 05+)

## Success criteria
A learner can:
1. Name the three FinOps phases and give one example of each
2. Explain why policy-as-prompt beats hard-coded rules for FinOps automation
3. Draw the Collect → Analyze → Act pipeline from memory
